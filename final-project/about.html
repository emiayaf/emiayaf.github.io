Biography

Soprano Emily Finke has performed the roles of Geraldine from Barber’s A Hand of Bridge and Noelle from Leanna Kirchoff’s Scrapbookers with Frost Opera Theater. She has also performed scenes as Tytania from Britten’s A Midsummer Night’s Dream, Tina from Dove’s Flight, and Gretel from Humperdinck’s Hänsel und Gretel. Most recently, Finke was a soloist with the Palm Beach Symphony in Gustav Mahler’s Symphony No. 4. Finke studies with Robynne Redmon at the Frost School of Music at the University of Miami and will graduate with a BM in vocal performance in May 2022. Emily will be pursuing a masters in vocal arts at the Bard Conservatory Graduate Vocal Arts Program.
